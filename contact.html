Frequently Asked Contact Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly do you respond?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. WhatsApp messages are typically answered within a few hours.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and WhatsApp. You can also contact us for updates.

How do I return an item?

Email us with your order number and reason for return. We'll guide you through the process within 14 days of delivery.

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for boutiques and resellers. Please contact us with your business details.
